Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how this site (“Grafana Desktop”) uses cookies and analytics. We host on GitHub Pages and link to official Grafana downloads. We are not affiliated with Grafana Labs.

What we collect

  • Strictly necessary cookies: a single cookie to store your analytics consent choice.
  • Optional analytics (Google Analytics 4): loaded only after you click “Accept analytics”. We send basic events like page views, download clicks, and “copy command” interactions. IP anonymization is enabled.
  • No personal accounts or forms: we do not collect names, emails, or payment data on this site.

Cookies

  • gd_cookie_consent — remembers whether analytics is allowed (granted) or declined (denied).
  • Analytics cookies are set by Google only after consent. You can revoke consent at any time using the button below.

Analytics

  • We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate usage (e.g., which pages help the most).
  • We enable anonymize_ip and avoid sending personally identifiable information.
  • You can opt out by declining analytics or using browser add‑ons that block tracking.

Third‑party links

We link to official resources (e.g., grafana.com). External sites have their own privacy policies. Review them before providing any data.

Data retention

Analytics data is retained according to our Google Analytics settings. Since we collect only basic, consented events, we keep retention to a reasonable minimum.

Your choices

  • Accept or decline analytics when prompted.
  • Revoke your choice anytime with the “Manage analytics consent” button.
  • Use browser privacy features or tracking protection if you prefer.

Security

We keep this site simple, avoid forms and server‑side processing, and rely on GitHub Pages. Still, no method of transmission is 100% secure on the internet.

Children’s privacy

This site is intended for technical audiences and not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date on this page.

Contact

Questions or requests? Open an issue at our GitHub repository (Issues tab).
